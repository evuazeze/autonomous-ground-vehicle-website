Autonomous Ground Vehicle (Priye)

by Alpha X

As part of the requirements for graduation from the Niger Delta University, the Autonomous Ground Vehicle (AGV) was built by a Team of Three - Bomanaziba Ogoni, Henry Ejemuta and Emmanuel Evuazeze my humble self.

The Autonomous Ground Vehicle nicknamed Priye is a wheeled robot that can navigate through short distances, detect edges and obstacles and avoid them.
